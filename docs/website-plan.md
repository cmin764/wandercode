**For**: Early-stage startups facing scaling and product-market-fit challenges

**Description**:
Not sure where to start with AI? Drowning in technical debt? I help founders and product leaders cut through the noise and build a clear path forward.

**What's Included**:

- AI readiness assessment - is your product ready for ML/LLM integration?
- Technical architecture review - identify bottlenecks and scaling risks
- Build vs. buy analysis - when to use off-the-shelf vs. custom solutions
- Technology stack recommendations - pragmatic choices, not hype-driven
- Product-market fit validation from a technical lens
- Roadmap creation with prioritized milestones

**Deliverables**:

- Written assessment report

**Ideal For**:

- Pre-seed to Series A startups

- Non-technical founders needing translation
- Teams considering their first AI feature

#### Service 2: AI Product Development

**For**: Existing or new products needing full-stack engineering with AI capabilities

**Icon**: Brain or Code

**Tagline**: "Confidence Driven Development"

**Description**:
I build products end-to-end - from database to deployment. My approach is **Confidence Driven Development**: shipping fast while maintaining the confidence that what you're shipping actually works. This means:

- Test coverage where it matters (not vanity metrics)
- Type safety and static analysis as guardrails
- Incremental delivery with working software at every stage
- Clear documentation that helps the next engineer (or future you)

**Technical Capabilities**:

- **LLM Integration**: OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, open-source models
- **RAG Pipelines**: Vector databases, embeddings, retrieval systems
- **Backend Systems**: APIs, microservices, async processing, queues
- **Frontend**: React/Next.js interfaces, dashboards, admin panels
- **Automation**: RPA, intelligent document processing, workflows
- **DevOps**: Kubernetes, Docker, CI/CD, monitoring, observability

**Engagement Models**:

- **Sprint-based**: 2-4 week focused engagements with defined outcomes
- **Embedded**: Join your team as a fractional team member (10-20 hrs/week)
- **Project-based**: End-to-end delivery of a defined scope

**Stack Preferences** (but language-agnostic):

- Python (FastAPI, SQLModel, pytest)

- PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch

#### Service 3: Workshops & Training

**For**: Teams adopting AI in day-to-day work

**Icon**: Users or GraduationCap

**Description**:
AI tools are only as good as the people using them. I run hands-on workshops that help your team actually ship faster - not just talk about AI.

**Workshop Topics**:

- **Claude Code & Cursor Mastery**: From prompt to production with AI-assisted coding

**Format Options**:

- Live workshop (2-4 hours, remote)

- Team office hours (ongoing advisory)

**What Teams Get**:

- Practical exercises with their own codebase
- Reference materials and cheat sheets
- Follow-up support for implementation questions
- Custom examples relevant to their domain

**Ideal For**:

- Engineering teams starting AI-assisted development
- Product teams wanting to understand AI capabilities
- Companies rolling out AI tools (Copilot, Claude, Cursor)
